Jake's Perfect Sandwich

Want a sandwich so perfect it'll make you cry? A sandwich to end all sandwiches? A sandwich worth slowing time for? Jake's Perfect Sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Instructions from the real of creation above you
  • Classical Music
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Boneless Ribeye Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Big Loaf of Bread
  • Cream Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Dill
  • Eggs
  • Bird from the Window
  • Cucumber
  • Roma Tomatoes
  • Yellow Onion
  • Tears
  • Bacon
  • Lobster

Instructions

  1. Wash your vegetables and boil some eggs.
  2. Make sure your knife is sharp.
  3. Season your steak with salt and pepper and then place inside your sous vide bag with rosemary and thyme. Sous vide at 135Â°F.
  4. Slice open your loaf of bread and toast with a blow torch.
  5. Spread cream cheese on both the halfs of the loaf.
  6. Put pickles on one half and dill on the other.
  7. Dice your boiled eggs and add to the sandwich
  8. Grab a bird from the window and cook it, adding it to your sandwich.
  9. Add some sliced cucumber, roma tomatoes and yellow onions.
  10. Wipe your tears from your wonderful creation and flick onto your sandwich for salt.
  11. Add your meat prepared sous vide.
  12. Fry some bacon and add to sandwich.
  13. Boil a lobster and waft its soul onto the sandwich
  14. Close your sandwich and revel at its incredible shimmer.
